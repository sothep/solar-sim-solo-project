PVRays uses the PVWatts Version 5 API, developed by the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL), to estimate of month-to-month energy performance for a solar-photovoltaic (PV) installation.

To get started, click Create New Solar Installation. You can simulate up to ten installations, choosing a variety of features such as location, orientation, and system power.

To view or remove your saved installations, select View Saved Solar Installations. Here you will find a graphical comparison of monthly energy estimates for your PV arrays.
